Welcome to the TankTrouble Online Competition

A competition made to sort out the best of the best TankTrouble players, by setting up a competition with multiple gamemodes to put your TankTrouble skills to the test! Players compete against each other in one-on-one single elimination style in each gamemode. The top three players recieve prizes to match their accomplishment, while the rest of the players and spectators recieve an exclusive tankcessory!

The Current Gamemode:

Classic

The all-round favourite, classic gamemode. Putting overall skill to the test, this gamemode is the easiest to follow: survive by any means necessary in the TankTrouble Mazes!

Gamemode Rules:

  • A normal battle with all the weapons and bullets
  • A round begins when Spectator Tanks are destroyed
  • To Win: 15+ wins, 2-win difference
