Gamemodes

TTOC is all about variety. That's why we have four gamemodes for players to compete in, to test players' skills. Each gamemode targets a different group of skills, while the Classic gamemode is an overall test. The gamemodes are played one after the other in TTOC, and they all have their own separate sign up period. Depending on the number of sign ups, the gamemodes can be played in either 16 or 32 player single-eliminations.

The Current Gamemode:

Classic

The all-round favourite, classic gamemode. Putting overall skill to the test, this gamemode is the easiest to follow: survive by any means necessary in the TankTrouble Mazes!

  • A normal battle with all the weapons and bullets
  • A round begins when Spectator tanks are destroyed

All bullets and weapons allowed

Tanks playing Classic after Spectators are destroyed

Weapons

Bullets are underkill. Destroy your opponent with the weapon of your choice. After all, that crate did spawn just for you! Go on... you know you want that weapon...

  • A battle without bullets, only weapons from crates
  • Both players start with only one weapon loaded
  • Player must pick up a weapon and "claim" a corner of the maze by announcing it in chat.
  • Players may pick up and use all weapons infinitely after the round starts
  • Players may only pick up and dispose of weapons to clear paths, and shields to allow spawns
  • A round begins when players are in corners, and Spectator tanks are destroyed

All weapons allowed, no regular bullets

Shields allowed only after match begins

Tanks starting in the farthest corners of the maze

Golf

Five bullets is overkill. Slow down! Aim from where you are. The target: your opponent, of course. If you miss... well then, now you're the target. You can dodge by turning, but it's not like you're good at it.

  • Players take turns shooting at each other on the spot with a single bullet until one dies
  • The first shooter alternates every match
  • Aiming and dodging is only allowed by pivoting on the spot
  • If a bullet despawns, the shooter will move to the location of the bullet to end their turn
  • Dispose of weapons if necessary
  • A round begins when Spectator tanks are destroyed

Only one bullet allowed at a time

Dispose of weapons to clear paths if necessary

Shooter aiming for their target

New for 2020!

Gold Rush

It's the 1840s. You're caught amidst the Siberian Gold Rush! You must fight for all the gold, or receive a face full of lead. Rush for the gold or lose, which one will you choose?

  • In Deathmatch, players must collect as much Gold coins and Dimitrium as possible
  • Gold coins are worth 1 point, Dimitrium is worth 3 points
  • Winning the match and Overtime points do not count
  • Prevent opponents from reaching gold with all bullets and weapons
  • Spectator tanks must be logged out to begin the match and prevent interference
  • First player to at least 15 points with a 2 point difference wins

All bullets and weapons allowed

You can destroy your tank to respawn closer to gold...
